> django-admin startproject mysite


In /mysite/mysite/settings.py the authentication application must be inside the list. The app is already installed by default.
	INSTALLED_APPS = (
	    'django.contrib.admin',
	    'django.contrib.auth',
	    'django.contrib.contenttypes',
	    'django.contrib.sessions',
	    'django.contrib.messages',
	    'django.contrib.staticfiles',
	)

> django-admin changepassword wasit
